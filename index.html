Privately Held Businesses

Our Practice

Operational consulting that works from within the business — not from a distance. Every engagement involves direct on-site involvement with leadership, financial data, and operational teams.

01
On-site presence We work embedded within the business — alongside leadership and operational teams — rather than in an advisory capacity from the outside.
02
Execution over reporting Engagements are focused on operational improvements that are implemented, measurable, and built to last — not delivered in a document.
03
Selective by design We limit the number of active engagements to ensure direct involvement in each. In select situations, we invest alongside the businesses we support.

About Divi Consulting

People · Processes · Profits

Since 1994, Divi Consulting has worked with private equity firms and privately held businesses to improve operational performance and execution across teams and locations.

Work spans portfolio companies, multi-location operators, and owner-led businesses requiring disciplined execution, accountability, and measurable performance improvement.

Engagements are selective to ensure direct involvement and operational focus across each client relationship.

People The right leadership structures and team alignment are the foundation of consistent operational performance.

Common Questions

How does an engagement typically start?

Most engagements begin with a conversation focused on your business, operational priorities, and goals. From there, we determine whether there is a strong fit for partnership.

How long does an engagement take?

Engagement timelines vary based on the scope and operational needs of the business. Some projects are short-term initiatives, while others evolve into ongoing operational partnerships.

Do you work remotely?

Some work can be handled remotely, though many engagements benefit from on-site involvement and direct collaboration with leadership and operational teams.

What makes you different from other consultants?

Our work is operationally focused and hands-on. We spend time within the business itself, working alongside leadership teams to support implementation and long-term improvement.
