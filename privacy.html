Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

Divi Consulting ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website operationsandprofits.com. This policy explains how we handle information collected through this site. We're a consulting firm, not a data company — so we keep this simple and honest.

Information We Collect

The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily provide through our contact form — specifically your name, email address, and any details you share about your business. We do not collect any other personal data.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact Form

Our contact form is powered by Formspree, a third-party form service. When you submit the form, your information is transmitted through Formspree's servers to our email inbox. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools. We do not monitor your behavior on this site or share browsing data with any third parties.

Third Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Any information you submit is done at your own risk.

Your Rights

You have the right to request that we delete any personal information you have submitted to us. To do so, contact us directly at paul@operationsandprofits.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at paul@operationsandprofits.com
